I'm presently pursuing a PhD in music theory at Yale University. I have a wide array of theoretical interests, often stemming from my own activity as a composer and improviser, but for the time being I have whittled them down to focus upon the computational analysis of music. My dissertation project, currently in its earliest stages, will apply deep learning to the analysis of dissonance treatment.
